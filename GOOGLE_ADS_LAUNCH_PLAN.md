# Google Ads Launch Plan

Prepared for Casa4 Developments lead generation.

## Goal

Generate quote enquiries for high-value outdoor work across the South Coast, with every form lead, phone click and WhatsApp click tracked.

Primary conversion:

- `generate_lead`

Secondary conversions:

- `phone_click`
- `whatsapp_click`
- `quote_cta_click`

## Pre-Launch Checklist

- GA4 receiving data from `G-HE2Q3YZRT0`.
- GTM container `GTM-NCRRT42P` published.
- Google Ads linked to GA4.
- `generate_lead` imported into Google Ads as the primary lead conversion.
- Phone and WhatsApp clicks imported as secondary conversions.
- Location setting uses people **in or regularly in** the target area, not people merely interested in it.
- Final URLs use service-specific landing pages.
- Search terms checked daily for the first week.
- Client confirms what counts as a payable lead before launch.

## Starting Budget

Use a small test budget first.

Conservative test:

```text
£15-£25 per day total
```

Better learning budget:

```text
£30-£50 per day total
```

Suggested split:

- 40% Block Paving
- 35% Patios
- 25% Landscaping

Pause any campaign that spends without producing useful enquiries after enough clicks to judge.

## Bidding

Start with:

```text
Maximize Clicks with a CPC limit
```

Then move to:

```text
Maximize Conversions
```

Only switch once conversion tracking is proven and the account has enough lead data.

## First 7 Days After Launch

Daily:

- Check search terms.
- Add irrelevant searches as negatives.
- Check spend by campaign.
- Check leads in D1 export and GA4.
- Check form, phone and WhatsApp conversion events.

After 7 days:

- Pause weak keywords.
- Move budget toward the best service.
- Add more local pages if searches show demand.
- Review lead quality with the client.
